1. Introduction

ScanView Pro (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who use our application and related services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the ScanView Pro mobile application and any associated services, including our integration with the Pinterest platform.

By using ScanView Pro, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the application immediately.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account or connect via Pinterest, we may collect your name, email address, username, and profile picture.
  • User Content: QR codes, barcodes, and scanned data that you generate or process within the app.
  • Communications: Information you provide when contacting our support team.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: Features accessed, time spent in the app, scan frequency, and interaction patterns.
  • Advertising ID: Android Advertising ID for serving personalized advertisements via Google AdMob.

2.3 Information from Pinterest Integration

When you authorize ScanView Pro to connect with your Pinterest account, we may access the following data through the Pinterest API v5, subject to your permission:

  • Public Profile Data: Pinterest username, profile image, bio, and website URL.
  • Board Information: Board names, descriptions, pin counts, and follower counts of your public boards.
  • Pin Data: Pin titles, descriptions, images, and engagement metrics (saves, comments, likes) for your public pins.
  • Analytics: Aggregate impression, click, and engagement data for your Pinterest content.

We only access data necessary to provide our Pinterest-integrated features. We do not access your private boards, private pins, or direct messages.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the Service: Operate the QR/barcode scanning and generation features, including batch processing and export tools.
  • Pinterest Integration: Display your Pinterest boards and pins within the app, generate content suggestions based on your Pinterest activity, and provide analytics on your Pinterest performance.
  • Improvement and Personalization: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features, personalize your experience, and develop new tools.
  • Advertising: Serve relevant advertisements through Google AdMob. The Android Advertising ID is used for interest-based advertising. You may reset or opt out at any time via your device settings.
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries, troubleshoot issues, and provide technical support.
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal requests.

4. Pinterest API Compliance

ScanView Pro complies with the Pinterest Developer Platform Terms of Service and Pinterest API Terms. Specifically:

  • We only request the minimum scopes necessary to provide our Pinterest-integrated features.
  • We do not sell Pinterest user data to third parties.
  • We do not use Pinterest data for targeting, retargeting, or interest-based advertising without explicit consent.
  • We do not store Pinterest access tokens beyond the duration necessary to provide the service.
  • We respect Pinterest user content deletion requests and synchronize deletions promptly.
  • We provide clear notice to users before accessing any Pinterest data via OAuth authorization screens.

App Review Status: ScanView Pro is a Pinterest Developer Platform application undergoing standard app review. All data access is subject to Pinterest's review and approval process.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share data with third parties.
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party vendors who assist in operating our service (cloud hosting, analytics, crash reporting). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Advertising Partners: Google AdMob receives your Advertising ID for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

  • Scan History: Stored locally on your device. You may clear it at any time through the app settings.
  • Account Data: Retained for the duration of your account. Upon account deletion, data is deleted within 30 days.
  • Pinterest Data: Pinterest-related data is stored only while your Pinterest integration is active and is deleted within 24 hours of disconnection.
  • Advertising ID: Not stored on our servers; processed by Google AdMob per their privacy policy.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS 1.3.
  • Secure OAuth 2.0 flows for Pinterest authentication with token encryption at rest.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only.
  • Anonymization of analytics data where feasible.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but will promptly notify users of any confirmed data breaches affecting their information.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service provider in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent at any time where we rely on consent to process your data.
  • Opt-Out of Advertising: Opt out of interest-based advertising via your device settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization).
  • Pinterest Disconnection: Disconnect your Pinterest account at any time through the app settings. All associated Pinterest data will be deleted within 24 hours.
