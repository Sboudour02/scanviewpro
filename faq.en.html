Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is ScanView Pro free to use?
Yes! All scanning and generation features are completely free. The app is supported by ads.
2. Does the app require an internet connection?
Scanning and generating codes works entirely offline. You only need internet to open scanned web links and to load ads.
3. Is my data private?
Absolutely. Your scanned content never leaves your device. Google AdMob may collect device identifiers for advertising, but we do not sell or share your personal data.
4. Where can I find my previous scans?
Your scan history is saved in the "History" tab at the bottom of the screen — the last 10 scans are stored.
5. How can I share a generated code?
After generating a code, tap the "Share" or "Save" button to send it or keep it in your gallery.
6. What barcode formats does the app support?
QR Code, Code 128, Code 39, EAN-13, UPC-A, Codabar, Data Matrix, PDF417, EAN-8, and ITF.
7. Can I create multiple barcodes at once?
Yes! Use Batch Generation to import a CSV or Excel file and generate up to 500 codes at once.
8. How do I save a generated code to my device?
Open the share menu and choose "Save to Gallery" or "Save to Files." No extra permissions needed.
9. Can I customize QR code colors or add a logo?
Yes, in the QR generator you can change foreground and background colors and add a logo image.
10. What should I do if the app crashes?
Make sure you're on the latest version. If the issue persists, contact us from the Help section.
