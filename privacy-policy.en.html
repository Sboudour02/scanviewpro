Privacy Policy – ScanView Pro

Last Updated: June 22, 2026

TL;DR

ScanView Pro's core scanning features (QR codes, barcodes) work entirely offline on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your scanned content, generated codes, or personal files. To keep the app free, we use Google AdMob for advertising, which may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We never sell your data.

1. Introduction

ScanView Pro ("we", "our", "us") is a tool application developed by e-Market Hub. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our app. Our core philosophy is privacy by design: the scanning, generation, and processing of QR codes and barcodes all happen locally on your device.

2. Data Collection & Usage

2.1 Core Scanning Features

We do not collect any personal data for our core features. Barcode and QR code scanning operates entirely offline on your device. No account is required, and we do not track your scan history, generated codes, or app activity. The camera feed is processed in real-time on your device and is never stored, recorded, or transmitted.

2.2 Google AdMob (Advertising)

To support the free version of the app, we integrate Google AdMob, a third-party advertising SDK. AdMob may automatically collect the following data types to serve and measure ads:

  • Device identifiers: Advertising ID (AAID on Android, IDFA on iOS)
  • App interactions: Ad impressions, clicks, and engagement
  • Crash logs & diagnostics: Performance and error data to improve ad delivery

This data is collected and processed by Google under their own Privacy Policy. AdMob may share this data with third-party ad networks for personalized advertising. We do not receive any personally identifiable information from AdMob beyond aggregate ad performance metrics.

For more details, see Google's Ads Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

2.3 File Access

The app only accesses files you explicitly choose:

  • Images you select from your gallery for scanning.
  • Storage location you choose to save generated codes or exported data.

We do not browse, read, or transmit any other files on your device.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. The only data sharing that occurs is through Google AdMob as described in Section 2.2, which is governed by Google's privacy practices.

4. Children's Privacy

Our core scanning features do not collect any personal information. However, because Google AdMob may collect device identifiers to serve personalized ads, children under the age of 13 (or the relevant age of consent in your jurisdiction) should use the app only with parental supervision. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data through our app, please contact us.

5. Data Security

Since your scanned content never leaves your device, its security depends on your device's built-in protections. We recommend keeping your operating system updated and using standard security measures (passcode, biometric lock).

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising via your device settings (Android: Settings → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization).
  • Reset your Advertising ID at any time through your device settings.
  • Contact us with questions about your data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our app or legal requirements. Updates will be communicated by changing the "Last Updated" date above and through app store release notes.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Help & Feedback section in the app, or email us at the address listed in the official app store listing.
